Awareness

Let's wake up!

Mental health problems are not new to this world, it has been present for a long time
and has been experienced by people in the past. This topic has been more talked about
now because it has greatly affected a lot of people. The pandemic is one of the biggest
reasons why the number of people experiencing this issue has greatly expanded. The isolation
and changes experienced by the world for the past two years have affected the mental health
of people. Students have experienced mental health problems because of the new
learning setup and adults have also experienced this issue because of the problems
brought on by the pandemic.

Let us try to help the people experiencing these problems by being aware
and not invalidating their feelings. There are many organizations that
are doing their best to help people experiencing this issue,
let us try to also help them and be part of the change.
